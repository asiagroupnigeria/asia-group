About Asia Cosmetics

Wholesale Beauty,
At Every Price Point

Asia Cosmetics is the beauty and personal care distribution arm of Asia Group of Companies — distributing soaps, pommades, skincare, hair care, and personal hygiene products from global and regional brand principals to Nigeria's vast consumer market.

With deep market penetration in Northern Nigeria and the Asia Group's unparalleled logistics infrastructure behind it, Asia Cosmetics reaches wholesale buyers, retail chains, market distributors, and individual stockists at scale.

Nigeria's beauty and personal care sector is one of Africa's fastest-growing consumer segments, and Asia Cosmetics is positioned at its distribution heart.
