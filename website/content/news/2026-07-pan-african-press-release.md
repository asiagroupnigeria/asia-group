**KANO, NIGERIA** — Asia Group of Companies Ltd, Northern Nigeria's leading vertically integrated distribution conglomerate, today announced a comprehensive pan-African expansion strategy aimed at establishing a strong operational footprint in Central and East Africa by the end of 2025.

Official press release outlining the timeline and targets for Asia Group's expansion into Central and East Africa. The strategic move is designed to leverage the company's 36-year track record in high-volume distribution to serve new, rapidly developing markets.

### Key Expansion Targets

The initial phase of the expansion will focus on establishing distribution hubs in Cameroon and Chad, followed closely by targeted market entries into East Africa. The company plans to replicate its successful multi-sector distribution model, encompassing FMCG, pharmaceuticals, commercial vehicles, cosmetics, and electronics.

"Our vision has always been continental in scale," stated Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar, Founder and Group Chairman. "We are now ready to export our standards of trust, integrity, and efficiency to neighboring nations, creating jobs and driving commerce across borders."
