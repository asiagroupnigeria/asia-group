In a major step forward for healthcare distribution, Asia Pharmacy has officially opened its new state-of-the-art logistics hub in Kano. This facility is designed specifically to handle temperature-sensitive pharmaceutical products with unprecedented efficiency and scale.

A deep look at the warehousing, logistics, and distribution network that makes Asia Pharmacy the region's leader in pharmaceutical supply. The new hub features advanced cold-chain storage capabilities and automated inventory management systems.

### Ensuring Healthcare Access

By centralizing our pharmaceutical distribution operations in this high-capacity hub, we are significantly reducing lead times for hospitals, clinics, and retail pharmacies across Northern Nigeria. This ensures that life-saving medications reach patients faster and in optimal condition.

The Kano Logistics Hub represents our unwavering commitment to vertical integration and our mission to elevate the standard of healthcare commerce in the region.
