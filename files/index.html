36 Years of Excellence

From a Market Stall
to a Continental Empire

1988

The Beginning

Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar, still in junior secondary school, starts trading polythene bags at Abubakar Rimi Market in Kano with ₦20,000 capital. The dual pursuit of western and Islamic education alongside business sets the foundation for a lifelong ethic of discipline and ambition.

1990s

Expansion into Sugar & Detergents

The business pivots from polythene bags into sugar trading, then transitions into the family core business of detergent distribution. A partnership with PZ Cussons — one of the world's leading FMCG companies — marks the beginning of Asia Group's rise to continental prominence. Asia Group becomes PZ Cussons' largest distribution partner in the region.

2003

Multi-Location Expansion

Operations expand across multiple locations within Abubakar Rimi Market and beyond. The foundation for a diversified conglomerate is laid as new product categories and partnerships are added. Distribution footprint begins to grow beyond Kano.

2010s

Subsidiary Formation

Formal incorporation of Asia Pharmacy, Asia Beverages, Asia Automobiles, Asia Cosmetics, and Asia Phones & Accessories as distinct subsidiary businesses under the Asia Group of Companies umbrella. Each vertical is positioned to lead its respective sector in Northern Nigeria.

2019

Head Office & Regional Dominance

Formal Head Office established in Niger State. Asia Group is recognised as the leading distribution partner for top global companies including Nestlé, Cadbury, Olam, Dangote Group, BUA Group, and others. Pan-African cross-border operations into Cameroon, Chad, and Niger are formalised.

2024+

Continental & Global Expansion

Expansion into Central and East Africa underway. Plans for global presence in Asia, Europe, and America in progress. Manufacturing ambitions announced: Pampas, pads, and detergent production facilities in development — moving Asia Group from pure distribution to integrated manufacturing and distribution.
