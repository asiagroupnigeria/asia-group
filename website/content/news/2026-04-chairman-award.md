Asia Group is proud to announce that our Founder and Group Chairman, Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar, has been honoured with the highly prestigious Trade Excellence Award at this year's African Commerce Summit.

Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar receives prestigious recognition for his contribution to continental commerce and trade development. The award highlights his 36-year journey from a modest market stall to building the most powerful distribution network in Northern Nigeria and beyond.

### A Legacy of Integrity

In his acceptance speech, the Chairman dedicated the award to the thousands of employees, partners, and community members who have been part of the Asia Group story. "This is not just a personal achievement; it is a testament to what we can build when we prioritize trust, integrity, and long-term vision," he remarked.

The recognition further fuels our ambition to expand our reach and positive impact across the African continent.
