The Group Chairman, Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar, announces a multi-year expansion strategy to cement Asia Group's position as Africa's undisputed distribution leader, targeting Central Africa, East Africa, and ultimately global markets in Asia, Europe, and America.

This marks a significant milestone in our history. From our origins in Kano, we are now extending our proven distribution model across borders, bringing trusted FMCG, pharmaceutical, and commercial vehicle solutions to rapidly growing African markets.

### Strategic Priorities for 2025

Our expansion will focus initially on establishing robust logistics corridors connecting Nigeria to key Central African hubs. By leveraging our existing partnerships with global manufacturers like Nestlé, Dangote, and BUA, we will replicate our successful model of deep penetration and extensive reach.

> "We want to build a legacy — a company that will stand the test of time, benefit our generation and future generations, and grow together with everyone involved."
> 
> — Alhaji Sani Isah Abubakar, Founder & Group Chairman

This expansion is expected to double our current distribution volume over the next five years, cementing our role as the bridge between global brands and African consumers.
