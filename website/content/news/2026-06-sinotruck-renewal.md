We are pleased to announce the renewal of our exclusive distribution agreement with SinoTruck, further cementing Asia Automobiles' position as the premier supplier of commercial vehicles and heavy machinery in Northern Nigeria.

A renewed and expanded partnership agreement positions Asia Automobiles as the primary distribution vehicle for SinoTruck's expanding commercial fleet across the region. This partnership ensures that construction, logistics, and industrial clients have uninterrupted access to world-class, durable machinery supported by our extensive after-sales service network.

### Powering Industry

The renewal of this agreement will see the introduction of new, more efficient truck models tailored specifically to the challenging terrain and demanding workloads of African logistics. By combining SinoTruck's engineering excellence with Asia Automobiles' unparalleled regional footprint, we are powering the industries that build our nations.
